Part 2 — Privacy Policy

4.0: Who We Are

Cosmos1562 (cosmos1562.space) is a satellite tracking service.

5.0: What Data We Collect

  • Account data: email address and hashed password when you register, or name and email when you sign in via Google or Yandex OAuth.
  • Usage data: your IP address (used to determine language preference), pages visited, and AI chat history within your session.

6.0: How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve the service
  • To authenticate your account and maintain your session
  • To determine the language of the interface based on your country
  • To enforce usage limits (AI message quota)

7.0: Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services which may process your data:

  • Google AdSense — displays advertisements on free-tier pages. Google may use cookies to serve personalised ads. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Yandex Adfox — displays advertisements for users in CIS countries. See Yandex Privacy Policy.
  • Google OAuth / Yandex OAuth — used for optional social sign-in.
  • OpenRouter AI — processes AI chat prompts. Prompts are sent to their API. See OpenRouter Privacy Policy.
  • Celestrak — satellite orbital data (no personal data shared).

8.0: Data Retention

Account data is retained for as long as your account is active. AI chat history is stored in your session only and is cleared when you close the chat or log out. You may request deletion of your account by contacting us.

9.0: Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us to exercise these rights.

Part 3 — Cookie Policy

10.0: Cookies We Use

  • Session cookie (essential): keeps you logged in during your visit. Required for the service to function.
  • CSRF token (essential): protects form submissions from cross-site attacks. Required for the service to function.
  • Advertising cookies (Google AdSense / Yandex Adfox): used by our advertising partners to serve relevant ads to free-tier users. These may track your activity across websites.

11.0: Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies will prevent you from logging in. You can opt out of personalised advertising via:

  • Google Ad Settings
  • Yandex Cookie Policy

12.0: Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
